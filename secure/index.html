Secure Contact

I value my privacy and informational security very highly, so below I have included various different ways for you to securely contact me.


Keybase

Keybase is a great online resource for sending messages with public-private key cryptography. To send a message to me, click the lock icon on the top right of the page. Then, send the generated message to me via any other contact method, and specify that you used my Keybase public keys to encrypt the message.

My Keybase Account

Ricochet

Ricochet is a wonderful peer to peer anonymous IM chat tool. All traffic is encrypted and sent via the TOR network. It is a completely decentralized service.

To send me a message on Ricochet, add me to your Ricochet contacts. Then, contact me via another service (preferably email) to organize a time for both of us to be online. Due to the decentralized nature of Ricochet, both members of a chat must be online to recieve messages.
